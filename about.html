About Page

In the middle of the bustling city, there was a dolphin named Denny. He had somehow found himself in the polluted river that ran through the city. At first, Denny struggled to adapt to his new surroundings, dodging boats and navigating through garbage.

But eventually, he learned to navigate through the murky waters and even made a few unlikely friends, like the ducks that waddled along the riverbanks. Denny became somewhat of a local legend, with people often stopping to watch him swim. Though he wasn't in his natural habitat, Denny made the most of his urban environment, and the city grew to love him.
